- **[Google Attributes Axios npm Supply Chain Attack to North Korean Group UNC1069](https://thehackernews.com/2026/04/google-attributes-axios-npm-supply.html)** — Google's Threat Intelligence Group attributed a March 31 supply chain attack on the Axios npm package — 100M weekly downloads, present in ~80% of cloud environments — to North Korea-nexus actor UNC1069. Attackers injected the WAVESHAPER.V2 backdoor via a malicious dependency "plain-crypto-js" in Axios versions 1.14.1 and 0.30.4 using a compromised maintainer account; the postinstall hook silently deploys a cross-platform backdoor targeting Windows, macOS, and Linux. Malicious releases were removed within three hours, but blast radius from the ~3-hour active window is unquantified. *Source: The Hacker News*

- **[D-Link NAS Devices Hit with Four CVSS 8.8 Vulnerabilities Across 20 Models](https://nvd.nist.gov/vuln/detail/CVE-2026-5211)** — Four HIGH-severity CVEs (CVE-2026-5211 through 5214, CVSS 8.8) were published targeting widely deployed D-Link NAS devices including DNS-120, DNS-320, DNS-320L, DNS-325, DNS-345, DNS-727-4, DNS-1100-4, DNS-1200-05, and DNS-1550-04. All four vulnerabilities are in separate handler functions (UPnP_AV_SETVAL, Webdav_UPnP, cgi_addgroup) and all are remotely exploitable; affected models are EOL with no patch available. *Source: NVD*

- **[XenForo Discloses OAuth2 Scope Bypass, Template Method Bypass, and Authenticated RCE — All CVSS 8.8](https://nvd.nist.gov/vuln/detail/CVE-2026-35056)** — Three HIGH-severity CVEs were published against XenForo forum software: CVE-2025-71278 (OAuth2 clients able to request unauthorized scopes, affects 2.3.x before 2.3.5), CVE-2025-71281 (loose template method prefix match allows unauthorized method invocations, before 2.3.7), and CVE-2026-35056 (authenticated admin RCE via template execution path, affects both 2.2.x before 2.2.18 and 2.3.x before 2.3.9). Admins should prioritize the RCE patch on any externally facing forum. *Source: NVD*
